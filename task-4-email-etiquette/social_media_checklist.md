# Social Media Usage Checklist

##  Do’s (Responsible Use)

- Use respectful and polite language in posts and comments  
- Verify information before sharing  
- Protect your privacy and personal information  
- Use social media for learning and networking  
- Maintain a positive and professional online presence  

---

##  Don’ts (Irresponsible Use)

- Do not post offensive or harmful content  
- Do not share fake news or misinformation  
- Do not share passwords or private details  
- Do not engage in cyberbullying or unnecessary arguments  
- Do not waste excessive time on social media
